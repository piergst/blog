## Pierre Giusti

Currently all-in on offsec and bug bounty. Before that, 10+ years as a developer: embedded and CNC-machine work on the industrial side, then desktop and web applications, with a fair amount of devops along the way. I like tech topics, searching, digging, learning, building my own tools.

I had another professional life too: timber framing, wooden boatbuilding, log cabins in Canada... A good few years putting together things that have to stand up, or float. That led me to designing a CNC machine for timber framing. I drew and built the first two prototypes alone, in a workshop; six years later the company was shipping a hundred machines a year.

In between, I sailed across the Atlantic.

Anyway, when a project pulls me in I have a hard time letting go :)
